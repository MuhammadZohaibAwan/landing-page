Blackberry Amber

The sweetness of blackberries combined with the sensual scent of amber produces a thoroughly enjoyable fragrance. Created with 100% all natural soy wax, these candles burn clean with very little soot. Soy wax, derived from soy bean oil, is a renewable resource unlike petroleum based paraffin wax. The soy wax is biodegradable, and any spills can be easily cleaned up with soap and water.
